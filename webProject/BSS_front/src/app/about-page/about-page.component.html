ABOUT US

Description of the restaurant "BSS's Restaurant" in Almaty:

Welcome to BSS's Restaurant, the perfect place for any occasion - whether it's a romantic date, a business meeting, a family dinner, or a celebration with friends. Our restaurant offers a cozy and welcoming atmosphere, where you can enjoy quiet evenings with loved ones or lively gatherings with a large group.

We have two halls to accommodate our guests - a large hall that seats up to 100 people and a smaller hall that seats up to 30 people. Our staff greets you with a warm smile and sincere hospitality, reflecting the best of Eastern hospitality.

At BSS's Restaurant, we take pride in our menu that caters to every taste. We offer a variety of traditional Oriental dishes, classic European cuisine, and Japanese seafood specialties. Our chefs use special recipes to create delicious and flavorful dishes, always prepared with passion and care.

But BSS's Restaurant is more than just a place to eat. We offer good prices, excellent service, and a warm and inviting atmosphere that will make you feel right at home. Come join us today and experience the best of Almaty's dining scene at BSS's Restaurant!
