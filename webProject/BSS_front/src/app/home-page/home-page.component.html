Arasan Restaurant:

Welcome to our cozy Kazakh restaurant! Here you will find delicious traditional dishes prepared with love and respect for our culture. Here you will feel at home - in an atmosphere of hospitality, comfort and true oriental cordiality.

We are the best:

We have real Kazakh cuisine, where each dish is made according to traditional recipes. We choose only fresh products, greet guests with a smile and take care of every detail. People come to us not just to eat, but to feel the warmth, the taste of childhood and the atmosphere of true hospitality.

Restaurant theme:

Our restaurant is a place where the traditions of Kazakh culture are observed. The interior is inspired by the steppe, yurts and national ornaments. We create an atmosphere of comfort, where everyone can feel part of a large Kazakh dastarkhan - with aromatic dishes, bright traditions and sincere hospitality.
